About

Our Mission

  • Promoting community, fitness, and fun through basketball at an affordable cost for those in need. Additionally, fostering a closer connection between Muslim youth and Islam.
  • Who it's for: Muslim youth (middle school and high school)
  • Why you started it: to give people who have less money a chance to have fun and play basketball and to socialize
